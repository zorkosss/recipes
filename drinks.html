Mocha Frappuccino

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ice
  • 80ml of Nescafe
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp of chocolate syrup

Steps:

  1. Mix all the ingredients together in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy!
