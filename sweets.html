Chocolate Cake Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • Vanilla
  • 100g (1⁄2 cup) sugar
  • 4 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2-3 tbsp hot milk
  • 60g (1⁄2 cup + 1 tbsp) flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Steps:

  1. Stir eggs, vanilla, and sugar together. Whip eggs until doubled and light in color.
  2. Bloom cocoa powder with hot milk and add to the mixture.
  3. Add flour, baking powder, and salt, then mix them together.
  4. Pour mixture onto a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake at 160°C (320°F) for 10 minutes.
  5. After baking, roll the cake with powdered sugar and cornstarch to prevent sticking.
  6. Allow to cool and enjoy!
Donuts

Ingredients:

  • 1⁄2 cup (112g) milk
  • 1 cup (140g) flour
  • 1 1⁄2 tsp baking powder

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients until the dough becomes sticky.
  2. Shape dough into small circles and fry until golden.
  3. Glaze the donuts and enjoy!
Chocolate Cupcakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a stand mixer, then add wet ingredients. Beat until smooth.
  2. Fill cupcake liners 2/3 full and bake at 160°C for 20-22 minutes.
  3. Check with a toothpick to see if they’re done and allow to cool.
Choux Pastry

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 8 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs

Steps:

  1. Bring water, milk, butter, sugar, and salt to a boil. Stir in flour.
  • Cook the dough over medium heat for about two minutes to release extra moisture.
  • Transfer the dough to a mixing bowl and cool it for about a minute.
  • Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition, until the dough is smooth.
  • Spoon the dough onto a baking sheet and bake at 425°F (218°C), then reduce the temperature to 160°C for about 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
