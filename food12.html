Stuffed Rigatoni Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Water and salt
  • Rigatoni
  • Olive oil
  • Ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp parsley
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup mozzarella
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup bread crumbs

Steps:

  1. Boil the rigatoni pasta with water and salt until done, then mix them with some olive oil and let them cool.
  2. Mix ground beef, salt, oregano, parsley, onion, garlic, and mozzarella together.
  3. Put the mixture in a freezer bag and squeeze it into the rigatoni.
  4. Freeze the rigatoni for 30 minutes.
  5. Coat the rigatoni with flour, eggs, and bread crumbs.
  6. Fry them with oil until golden brown.
  7. Enjoy your delicious stuffed rigatoni!
Gnocchi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 potatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 1-1 1/2 cups flour
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Boil the potatoes, peel them, then smash them. Add salt, pepper, and one egg, and mix them.
  2. Take the dough out of the bowl and add the flour, then knead it until it forms a dough.
  3. Shape the dough into desired shapes and boil them until they float.
  4. Fry them with butter, and they're done.
Caesar Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves (crushed)
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • French baguette (1 or any other bread)
  • 1/2 head iceberg lettuce
  • Salt to taste
  • 1-2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp thyme

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil and butter in a pan and cook the garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken breast and cook until fully cooked, then set aside and slice.
  3. Toast the French baguette (or any bread) in the pan, then cut it into crouton-sized pieces.
  4. Mix the mayonnaise, parmesan cheese, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper in a bowl to make the dressing.
  5. Tear the iceberg lettuce into bite-sized pieces and place it in a bowl.
  6. Add the chicken, croutons, and dressing to the lettuce, and toss everything together.
  7. Enjoy your Caesar salad!
Garlic Parmesan Potato Wedges

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Mandazi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted
  • Oil for frying

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and cardamom.
  2. Add the coconut milk, warm water, egg, and melted butter. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes, then cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface and cut into small triangles or squares.
  5. Heat oil in a pan or deep fryer to 350째F (175째C) and fry the dough pieces until golden brown and crisp.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot!
